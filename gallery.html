PHOTOGRAPHY

The photo is the hunt. It’s the instinct of hunting without the desire to kill. It’s the hunt of angels...
You track, you aim, you fire and click!

Instead of a dead man, you make him eternal.

Since I found those words, they became my manifesto. I understood that my mission is not just to see, but to seize the rawness of the moment trapping the visceral truth of the now to keep it alive until the end of days.

Life is fast, noisy, and absolute. I don’t want to fix it; I just want to frame it.
