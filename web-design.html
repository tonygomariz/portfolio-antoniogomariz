Ango Metalurgica

A comprehensive brand and web design project for Ango Metalurgica, a metallurgical company based in Argentina. The project encompassed logo design, website development, technical sheet creation, and product photography.

The client needed a professional digital presence that would showcase their industrial products while providing technical information in an accessible format. I created a clean, modern website with an intuitive navigation system that highlights their product catalog and specifications.

The project also included product photography sessions to capture the metalwork quality and detailed technical sheets that communicate precise specifications to potential customers.
